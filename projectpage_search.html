Adapted Search experience

what is this about
We upgraded IKEA's standard search pages to semantic search experiences by predicting the searcher intent and understanding the query context.

Client/team IKEA
Project Date 2021/22
Visit ikea.com
Skills UX research
UX/UI
Product strategy
Workshop facilitation



Challenge
Search engines based on keyword matching can return unexpected results on those sesions looking for inspiration and guidance.

This was the case of the old IKEA's search engine. In the aprox 20% of the search sessions metrics like the query rewrite were much higher than usual which indicated that customer weren't finding meaningful answers.




Main goal
Become more relevant by understanding and predicing customer's goals.

The goal was to obtain a way to automate the selection of different elements and layout that would support each need.Triangulasion of different qualitative and quantitative techniques to define those needs. Translate customers' needs into data dimensions the search engine can be trained with and later predict. As a result we can automate the selection of elements and personalise layouts to support those needs.

Explore


I completed an explorative research using recent related internal researches, Analytics and remote user testing. The learnings were compiled in a hypothesis model presenting a relation between certain group of queries and general user goals.





Align with engineering


The hypothesis model was presentend to engineers and business so the full team could challenge the learnings and agree on next steps. While data sciences and engineers trained the algorithm to predict and add the intent as metatags into the collected behavioral data , I built a validation plan with a second UX peer and a data analyst. The plan included remote moderated interviews and Analytics.

The challenge: the seasonal nature of some intents

The seasonal character of certain behaviours represented a blocker to complete the research on a timely manner. We reached sales SMEs to get suggestions on which commercial activity to focus on and reprioritise the research work.


Focus

Customers visit ikea.com trying to get answers so the more we are able to predict their intent, the more relevant we will be for them.




Ideation and prototype

I planed and facilitated ideation workshops including SMEs, SEO and the team to ideate and prioritise work. We involved engineers from the very begining of the prototyping and testing phase which helps to co-creating and discover potential technical blockers earlier.



Testing

The prototiping phase we select testing techniques, define hypothesis and metrics. We used A/B testing and unmoderated usability tests, targeting specific behaviours.
